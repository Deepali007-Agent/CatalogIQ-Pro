# 🚀 CatalogIQ Pro

## AI-Powered Retail Operations Intelligence Platform

CatalogIQ Pro is an Executive Retail Operations Command Center that transforms raw catalog data into actionable business intelligence.

The platform helps retail leaders identify revenue risk, catalog quality issues, vendor performance gaps, customer experience concerns, and recovery opportunities—without requiring access to ERP, CRM, or enterprise reporting systems.

Instead of simply identifying data issues, CatalogIQ Pro translates operational gaps into executive recovery actions and estimated business impact.

---

# Business Problem

Retail organizations often struggle with:

* Poor catalog quality
* Missing product attributes
* Inconsistent product content
* Vendor performance issues
* High return rates
* Low customer ratings
* Limited visibility into revenue exposure

Operational teams frequently identify problems but lack a framework to prioritize recovery actions based on business impact.

CatalogIQ Pro bridges this gap by converting operational signals into leadership-level decision intelligence.

# Core Capabilities

## 📊 Executive Summary

Provides leadership visibility into:

* Catalog Health
* Revenue At Risk
* Expected Recovery Opportunity
* Vendor Risk Exposure
* Customer Experience Risk
* Top Operational Risks
* Recommended Executive Actions

Outputs a leadership-focused recovery narrative rather than operational metrics alone.

---

## 🏷️ Catalog Health Engine

Evaluates catalog quality across:

### Catalog Completeness

Measures mandatory field completion.

Examples:

* Product Name
* Category
* Brand
* Description
* Attributes

### Content Quality

Identifies:

* Missing descriptions
* Weak content
* Incomplete PDP information

### Search Readiness

Measures discoverability through:

* Product titles
* Categories
* Search tags

### Attribute Completeness

Validates:

* Color
* Material
* Size
* Buying attributes

---

## 🏢 Vendor Intelligence Engine

Identifies vendor-driven operational risk.

Measures:

* Revenue Exposure
* Products Impacted
* Defect Rate
* SLA Compliance
* Return Rate
* Vendor Risk Level

Outputs:

* Vendor Risk Scorecard
* Vendor Recovery Opportunities
* Executive Vendor Recommendations

---

## 😊 Customer Experience Command Center

Measures customer-facing operational risk.

Evaluates:

### Return Risk

High-return products

### Rating Risk

Low-rated products

### Review Signal Risk

Products with weak customer feedback

Outputs:

* Customer Risk Drivers
* Revenue Exposure
* Recommended CX Actions

---

## 💰 Revenue Recovery Command Center

Converts operational gaps into financial recovery opportunities.

Measures:

* Revenue At Risk
* Expected Recovery
* Recovery Rate

Recovery opportunities include:

### Vendor Governance Recovery

Vendor-related operational exposure

### Customer Experience Recovery

Returns and rating-driven exposure

### Catalog Content Recovery

Content and attribute-driven revenue risk

### Division Recovery Program

Business-unit level recovery opportunities

---

## 📈 Executive Recovery Scenarios

Models multiple recovery outcomes.

### Conservative Recovery

30% recovery assumption

### Base Recovery

Current modeled recovery

### Aggressive Recovery

70% recovery assumption

Allows leadership teams to evaluate best-case and worst-case recovery outcomes.
